Faded Films - 12 Film & Retro Effects

Using the Photoshop Actions:

  1. Run the *.zxp file and follow the instructions.
  2. Go to your Actions panel (Window > Actions), open the panel menu located on the top-right of your panel, then choose "Faded Films by SparkleStock".
  3. In your Actions panel (Window > Actions), select the "Render All Effects" action then click the play button.
  4. The effects will appear as snapshots in your History panel (Window > History). Select the effect that you'd like to use.

OR

  1. Go to Window > Actions. Click on the panel menu (top-right corner of the panel) then click Load Actions. Browse for the "Silver by SparkleStock.atn" file then click Load.
  2. In your Actions panel (Window > Actions), select the "Render All Effects" action then click the play button.
  3. The effects will appear as snapshots in your History panel (Window > History). Select the effect that you'd like to use.
