Instructions

1.Click download. 
2.Unzip the .rar file on your computer. 
3.Open Photoshop. 
4.On the “Window” tab, click “Actions”, once the window has opened you will see an arrow pointing right in the top right hand corner, click this then click “Load Actions”. 
5.In the end you just have to choose your action and click the play button.
